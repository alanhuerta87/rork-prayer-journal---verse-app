Privacy Policy for My Prayer Journal

Last updated:

Introduction

My Prayer Journal ("we," "our," or "the App") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy describes how we handle information in our mobile application that includes prayer journaling and Bible reading features.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

When you use My Prayer Journal, the following information is collected and stored locally on your device only:

Account Information:

  • Email address
  • Name
  • User ID (automatically generated timestamp)
  • Account creation date

User-Generated Content:

  • Prayer entries (title, content, category, tags)
  • Prayer dates and timestamps
  • Favorite prayer markings
  • Bible reading bookmarks and last reading position
  • Bible translation preferences
  • App theme preferences (light/dark mode, color themes)

Technical Information

  • Basic error logging (console only, not transmitted)
  • Bible verse cache data (temporary, for performance)
  • User interface preferences

How We Store Your Data

All data is stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage. We do not:

  • Use cloud storage or remote servers
  • Sync data across devices
  • Backup your data to external servers
  • Use real backend authentication (authentication is local/mock only)

Third-Party Services

Bible Content APIs

The App uses the following APIs solely to retrieve Bible content:

  • bible-api.com
  • labs.bible.org

Important: These services only receive:

  • Book names
  • Chapter numbers
  • Verse numbers

No personal information is ever sent to these Bible API services.

What We DON'T Do

  • No Analytics or Tracking: We don't use Google Analytics, Facebook SDK, or any tracking services
  • No Crash Reporting: We don't use services like Crashlytics
  • No Advertising: No ad networks or advertising identifiers
  • No Social Media Integration: No data collection through social platforms
  • No Data Sharing: We never sell, trade, or share your personal information

Your Control Over Your Data

  • All data remains on your device under your complete control
  • Deleting the app removes all associated data permanently
  • There is no cloud backup or sync feature
  • You can export your data using your device's built-in features

Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device, the security of your information depends on your device's security measures. We recommend:

  • Using device lock screens
  • Keeping your device's operating system updated
  • Being cautious about who has physical access to your device

Children's Privacy

My Prayer Journal does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. Since all data is stored locally and we don't transmit personal information, parents have full control over their children's use of the app on their devices.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
